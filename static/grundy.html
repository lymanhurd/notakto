Grundy's Game

First, set up the desired number (three minimum) of stones by using the plus/minus buttons. When you have the number you want, start the game by making a move. The computer will then make its move.

A move consists of breaking a group of stones into two groups with an unequal number of stones. You divide a group by clicking on the dot to the right of the dividing point.

For example, if you select the third dot of a group of five, you will divide that group into a group of two and a group of three). The computer distinguishes groupd by changing colors.

The last player who can make a move, wins. Note you cannot click on a group with one or two members.
