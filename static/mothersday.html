Mother's Day 2017

Happy Mother's Day! I collected info from several different family members about how awesome you are. I hope you enjoy!

Glenn says:

Mom means a lot to me. Whenever I have a problem that needs to be solved she is there. She always takes time to talk with me about important things such as work, exercise, and even some card games that I play with friends. She is hard working and is an excellent role model for me. Despite all the silly jokes I have told her over the years she still laughs at all the funny ones I say today. This and more is why I love Mom.
